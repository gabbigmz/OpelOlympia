A car for life!

By: Gabriela Gomez

Have you ever wondered how it is to have the same car for a looooong period of time?

Meet Ollie. A classic beauty from 1960. Ollie is an Opel Rekord Olympia.
I've been asked so many times if I would exchange it for a brand new car, however, my answer remains the same; Once you meet Ollie, there's no turning back.


Reasons to have a Classic car :

  1. They're very simple to maintain.
  2. They don't have complicated computers that look like Christmas lights when something isn't working properly.
  3. Low maintenance costs.
  4. Everybody wants a picture with them!
